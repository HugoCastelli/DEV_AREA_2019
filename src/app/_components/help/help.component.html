What is causing you problems?
AREA

Area is a free web / mobile service allowing its users create simple instruction strings called applets. An applet is triggered by changes that take place within web services such as Gmail, Facebook, Instagram or Pinterest.

 How does AREA works ?

• Create a free account.
• Browse the AREA website or app to find an Applet that interests you.
• Click into the Applet and turn it on.
• Connect the services that are involved in the Applet — this is only so we can use them to run Applets on your behalf. AREA provides a layer between different services to only allow them to do what you specifically tell them to do.
• Find more Applets, and repeat!

 How to sign up ?

You can sign up for AREA via the website by visiting AREA.com/join.

You can also sign up by downloading the AREA app from the App Store or Google Play.

You can sign up for AREA with your email, or you can connect with Google or Facebook.

 How to delete my account ?

AREA account
To delete your user information from our databases, follow the instructions below.

• Log into your account settings.
• Click on account management (application) and [DO] (web)
• Click on delete my account.

 What is an Applet ?

An Applet connects two or more apps or devices together. It enables you to do something that those apps or devices couldn't do on their own.
Applets are composed of triggers and actions

Triggers tell an Applet to start, and actions are the end result of an Applet run

To use an Applet, you'll need a free AREA account. Then you need to connect your apps and devices to AREA, so we can help them talk to each other.

 How do I edit an Applet ?

Select the ⚙︎ icon on the top right corner of the Applet to edit its settings.
Once you've edited your Applet, select Save to update it.

 How do I turn off or delete an Applet ?

Turning Applets off:

Visit AREA.com or the mobile app
Select the service of the Applet or connection you'd like to turn off
Select the Applet or connection and flip the switch


Deleting Applets:

Locate the Applet
Select the ⚙︎ icon on the top right of the Applet
Scroll to the bottom to select Delete
